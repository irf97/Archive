ENS Navigator Product Spec

A local-first research operations app for expert discovery, domain knowledge curation, and outcome execution. ENS Navigator keeps structured data on the local machine while exposing a browser UI and local API for agents.

Current app: index.html
Backend: server.mjs
Store: data/*.json
URL: http://127.0.0.1:8787/

Mission

ENS Navigator turns research activity into a navigable operating system: find experts, organize knowledge, review claims, and convert domains into executable outcome graphs.

local-first source-grounded agent-ready human review loop no unsupported memory promotion

Application Pages

Navigator

Tracks expert-network engagements, payments, status, follow-ups, and exported CSV records.

Expert Radar

Catalogs candidate experts, applies structured tag criteria, supports saved views, selection, bulk actions, and pipeline movement.

Domain Knowledge

Stores domains, claims, sources, people, questions, links, review lanes, researched findings, and hypotheses.

Outcomes

Turns ingested domains into task graphs with evidence gates, dependencies, remediation tasks, and exportable project JSON.

Ingest Queue

Shows recent local API ingestion and crawl jobs for operational visibility.

Core Entity Rules

  • Claims must retain sourceIds and review status.
  • Sources should identify type: paper, RFC, official doc, dataset, institutional page, or other public source.
  • Outcome tasks should include sourceIds or claimIds before they can be marked Done.
  • Rejected or disputed knowledge can still spawn Outcomes, but those tasks should be remediation or pressure-test tasks.

Primary Workflows

1Find

Use Expert Radar tags, saved views, and ingestion tools to collect relevant people.

2Research

Ingest source-backed Domain Knowledge: claims, sources, questions, people, links.

3Review

Move knowledge into Approved, Needs Source, Disputed, Rejected, or Hypotheses.

4Execute

Create Outcome tasks from questions, approved claims, and disputed/needs-source remediation work.

5Loop

Blocked Outcomes spawn research jobs or expert review packets, then update Domain Knowledge.
