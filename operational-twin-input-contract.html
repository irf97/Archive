Operational Twin Input Contract v1

This contract defines the bridge between ENS research, ARGOS approvals, an operational site reality graph, and Robodex execution inputs.

ENS does not send unsourced research claims directly to Robodex. ENS supplies evidence-linked candidates. ARGOS approves or rejects them. The Operational Twin turns accepted site reality into explicit Robodex forge inputs.

Layer Responsibilities

ENS NavigatorDiscovers sources, people, claims, hypotheses, questions, and outcome tasks.
ARGOSOwns approval state, canon, operator policy, and promotion gates.
Operational TwinRepresents the accepted physical, hardware, software, authority, workflow, and runtime state of a site.
RobodexCompiles or refuses execution cartridges from validated twin-derived inputs.
Convergent CellSynchronizes events, trust, proof updates, and divergent local projections.
Proof EventsFeed execution evidence back into ARGOS memory and twin runtime state.

Promotion Gates

Draft

Operator-created or imported site projection. It may contain assumptions and cannot drive execution.

Review

Linked to approved ENS claims or operator-provided field observations. Still requires ARGOS approval.

Approved

Accepted as a current site projection. Robodex may receive generated forge inputs, but runtime can still refuse unsafe missions.

Active

Connected to live proof events, telemetry, or site updates.

Needs Source / Disputed

Blocked from execution promotion until missing evidence or contradiction is resolved.
